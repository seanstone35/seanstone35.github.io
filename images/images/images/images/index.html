About BNN Piano

With roots in Baltimore's musical community, BNN Piano was founded by technicians and performers who believe every piano should inspire.

Our showroom features rotating collections of new, pre-owned, and fully restored instruments. We partner with recital halls, educators, and families to ensure each piano is matched to its setting.

Visit us for a private audition, explore financing options, or let us bring select models to your venue for a trial.
