Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2024

Overview

BNN Piano respects your privacy. This policy describes the types of information we collect when you interact with our website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact form submissions: When you submit a form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any additional information you provide.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: Your email address is collected to send updates and promotions.
  • Website usage data: We may collect aggregated analytics data to improve our services.

How We Use Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Process service requests and product holds.
  • Send newsletters or marketing communications when you opt in.
  • Improve our website, inventory selection, and overall customer experience.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers who assist with website operations, email delivery, or logistics, and only as necessary to perform their services.

Cookies & Analytics

BNN Piano may use cookies or similar technologies to analyze website performance. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though this may impact certain features.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of marketing communications by using the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Request updates or deletion of your data by contacting privacy@bnn-piano.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
